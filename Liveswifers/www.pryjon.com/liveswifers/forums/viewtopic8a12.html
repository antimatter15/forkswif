I thought I would put a little “resource” together for possible ways of making money off your game. First off there are three main ways to make money off you game; contests, where you compete against other games in some way, money depends on the website. Sponsorships, they can either mean you game is site-exclusive or you have a site preloader and in-game site ads for the website, money depends on the website. Ad-revenue, is when said website gives back money earned off your game to you, money depends on the website.


I know most games made with LiveSwif are probably not going to be considered 'good' enough to make any real money, but I think it's nice to see what's out there.

If there's a site you would like added in, post it here, or PM me using the forums PM system and I'll give you full credit for whatever you submit, thanks.
Helpful sites for learning your best options

flashgamesponsorships.com: a developer’s guide http://www.flashgamesponsorship.com/


ArcadeStreet.com

Contests: None, that I could find.

Sponsorships: anywhere from $100 to $1200 depending on quality More info here http://www.arcadestreet.com/game-sponsorship.aspx

Ad-revenue: None, that I could find.

ArmorGames.com

Contests: They have challenges from time to time which pay quite well, so look for them.

Sponsorships: they pay very well on sponsorships anywhere from $1500 to $7000!

More information about ArmorGames can be found here http://armorgames.com/developers-corner

Ad-revenue: none

CrazyMonkeyGames.com

Contests: none

Sponsorships: Yes, big time! They offer many sponsorships and where in price from a hundred or two, to the high thousands! Sponsorship page http://armorgames.com/developers-corner

Ad-revenue: none


Kongregate.com

Weekly contests (decided by user ratings)

1st-$250
2nd -$150
3rd-$150

Monthly contests (decided by user ratings)
1st-$1500
2nd-$1000
3rd-$700
4th-$400
5th-9th-$250

The problem is you have to have kongregate’s API in your game to clam the mothly and weekly contest money, which there is no AS1 API, so that is pretty much unwinnable using LiveSwif

Sponsorships: Yes, Kongregate does sponsor games, look here for more info http://www.kongregate.com/pages/help#works
And you can contact them here with sponsorship proposals sponsor(at)kongregate.com

Ad revenue: Kongregate shares ad revenue with developers, the amount varies from 25 to 50% depending on whether it’s site exclusive, Kongregate sponsored, and whether it has API. More info can be found here http://www.kongregate.com/pages/help#revenue


Nonoba.com

Weekly contest(most plays within a week is the winner)
1st-$500

Monthly contest(most plays within a month is the winner)
1st-$2500

Again you need to have their API which is only AS2 and AS3 compatible. They are also having a multiplayer API contest, best game with their new multiplayer API wins $25,000. WHY NO AS1 API, WHY?!?!
More info here http://www.nonoba.com/developers/contests/

Sponsorships: None of this time

Ad revenue: unless major site changes happen, it will never happen.
