Thanks, ShaneP, I have been thinking about (and dreading!) how to adjust the kerning... before I do though, does anyone have an idea how to do it? (I am not going to tell my idea until I get a few responses, otherwise I will get about 5 people saying "Oh yeah, that's about the only thing I can think of... yeah that would probably work..." and not get any other ideas.)

P. S. For those who don't know, the "kerning" is the spacing between the letters. Right now I have each letter spaced an equal distance from each other, but normal text has different widths for different letters. The biggest example is the lower case letter "i" or "l" which is just a very short width versus the upper case "W" or "M" which are very wide. A properly kerned textbox would take this into account.
