I do have some guidelines, though:

  • If it is bigger than 400x400 (i.e. 401x401, 403x203, or 250x650), please post a link instead to avoid dial-up people hanging-up.
  • Avoid neon colors (please, neon colors on a computer screen, especially against this black board, can cause severe burning of the retinas).

If you have good ideas for more guidelines, then PM me about it (it will save the database space).
