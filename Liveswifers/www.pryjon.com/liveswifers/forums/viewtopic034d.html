this tutorial covers looping a movie alone in 3 steps. the looping means playing the movie over and over, not including the preloader. it is wise to include a button to stop and take you back to the preloader.

to loop a movie:
1.open liveswif and make an animation.
2.click on the last frame of the animation.
3.put this code on the frame:
Code:
ifFrameLoaded(x);
gotoAndPlay(frame after preloader);


the "x" stands for the last frame in the movie's number.
the "frame after preloader" should be replaced with the number of the frame after the preloader.
