jeremy1080 wrote:
cool is she a regular on the forum or does she just come once in a while

I generally try to keep this forum clean (although I do need to post more), but once in a while I do make up an animation or something like that (right now I'm working on a Liveswif tutorial).

jeremy1080 wrote:
We could just have one person work on it who is real good at liveswif o show what liveswif can do for free

In my opinion, it's much, much better to have a decent amount of good-quality Liveswif-made flashes from everyone on Liveswifers (or at least, the regulars) to the NG portal, instead of having just one "spokesperson" of the community submitting every so often. There are so many people on the Internet who would love to be able to dabble in the art of animation, but being hobbyists, can't afford Flash. Everyone on Liveswifers used to be one of them.

I'm not an expert of animation, and I can't say that Liveswif can make the next Waterlollies (it doesn't even have a brush tool!), but the point is that everyone should always try to improve your animation and art skills since in a way, you're representing the Liveswif community when you submit a Liveswif-made Flash to the portal.

We all need to take care in making your games/animations and make them good enough to convince some of those aspiring animators that they really don't need to spend hundreds just to be able to take their first steps in digital animation, whether they intend to animate as a hobby or intend to move onto Flash as a career. Having only one person as the "spokesperson" won't achieve this as well as having everyone try their best.
