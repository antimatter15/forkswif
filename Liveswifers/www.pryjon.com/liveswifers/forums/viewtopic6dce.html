Simple. When you want to create the mouse-over item, just make a new button and draw the item. Then, copy the object, select the "Over State" layer in the Timeline, paste the object and change it slightly. This altered object will serve as the mouse over.

Peace.


When you create a new button, the layers in the Timeline should have different names. Selecting the layer dictates what state you're altering; having nothing on the layer means that the state won't be present in the final Flash.

Making buttons is easy, all you need are the Shape, Curve and Text tools. Just make your button just like you would make any other object in Liveswif. Just remember to make a slightly different version (i.e. differet colours) for each state/layer you want to use.
