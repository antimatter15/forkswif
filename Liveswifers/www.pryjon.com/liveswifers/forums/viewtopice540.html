Try this nifty experiment that will solve your problems.

First create a sprite of a box that has its four sides labeled up, down, left and right.

Then drag it onto the stage and use path motion on it.

Notice the right side of the box follows the path.

An easy way to visualize this as if the right side is the front of a car, and how it follows the road (the path in path motion).



Final: Try rotating the object inside the sprite.
