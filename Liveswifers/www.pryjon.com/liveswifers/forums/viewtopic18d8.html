This a list of Liveswifer recommended reading, movies, games, basically whatever we like! The main purpose of this list is to give each other ideas, and hopefully keep some creativity flowing!



Adding a Movie
Please give a Name,description, and a trailer if possible

Adding TV show/Tv episode
For just a TV show please give the Name,description, list of channels the show can be viewed on, and a trailer/episode if you want

Adding a book or comic
Please give the name of the book,name of the author, and a description about what it is about

Adding a Game
Please give a Name,description, and a trailer if possible


Adding a Flash Movie or Game
Please Give a link to the game or movie, a description of it, and the name of the author would be very nice










Movies
Animation
Tekkonkinkreet
- Two boys in the bad part of town, very philosophical and deep. (Original movie, with subtitles is recommended)

The Iron Giant
- A giant robot falls from the sky, can society handle it? Great animation!


Mind Game
-A man kills a gangster, and is now on the run! Very weird, but SO worth watching! (There is no Official English version, that I know of.)

We are the Strange
-Wow.....uh, words cant describe this film. Something new and interesting, the whole movie can be viewed on You Tube. You will either love it, or just be completely confused.

A Scanner Darkly
-This is an odd one, I am pretty sure its rotoscpoped, so I am not sure you can call it animation but whatever...


Waking Life
-Another film by the guys who made A Scanner Darkly. This is a weird one, and the art may make you feel kind of sea sick at first. It has a lot to do with dreams, and just weird theories that people come up with, some of which will feel very familiar, while others you will not understand at all.




Live Action





Television
What A Cartoon!: Tales of Worm Paranoia
-Just a great cartoon by Eddie Fitzgerald.




Books and comics
"The Metamorphosis"-By Franz Kafka
Dude turns into a bug, what more needs to be said?

"The Alex Rider Series"-By Anthony Horowitz
A series about a teenage boy becomes a spy.








Games

Perfect World
- Perfect World is a MMORPG, think of it as.....the poor man's WoW.Its free has UNBELIEVABLE character customization, and you can fly around on stuff. For a free to play game it is really fun. (If you play PM me your user name and server maybe we can play together )






Flash Movies and Games
Movies
HomeStarRunner
-Lol.....Just....Lol. Watch their other stuff,and maybe play their games.

Games




Music

Red Snapper
-These guys are a weird bunch, they play trip-hop. Its not a genre I listen to much, but I like these guys.








Other
"The Hunger" Part 1 Part 2

-This is a piece of history in my mind, it was made in 1974, and is the first computer animation.

Pose Maniacs
-If your having trouble learning how to draw the human form, I recommend this. Basically you just pick a time limit, and skitch the figure it shows you. You will feel pretty weird at first but trust me, this thing works really well!
