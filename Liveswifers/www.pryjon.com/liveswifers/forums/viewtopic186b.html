Time for a little tutorial that aint going about Liveswif 2.2
This time, it's all about cleaning your PC, and scan your PC From Malicious Ware.

First of all, let's start with the beginning.
Notice: All these software, are freeware.

Let's clean out your computer, of software you dont use.

This is a easy one. You go to the Windows Configuration window, and then
you go to Software. A list will be loading, and you select one of the software you want to be uninstalled, and then click on Delete.

Now the temporary files!

For this trick, we are going to use the software called Ccleaner. Ccleaner is a free program that cleans out your temporary files, both of windows, and internet explorer and FireFox.
(Download it here.

As soon as you start up, the screen is already loaded, and your off to go.
Select the Run Cleaner button, and it will clean up. When it's done, a nice report will be shown, how much space of temporary files are deleted.

Now, defraging it.

What is defragmentation? It's a proces on your computer, wich puts everything to it's normal place, so it's easier and faster for the computer to reach it.

For example: You have 4 numbers, 1 2 3 and 4. They are in the exact thing they appear. But fragments are like that al the numbers, are switched from places. So, like 3, 1, 4, and 2.
It's harder for the computer to reach it, since it's not in it's place.
With defragmentation we can fix this.

Windows has a defragmentation program itselfs, but we are going to use a more effective and faster defragmentation program. Called Auslogics Disk Defrag (Download it here.

Select one of the drives you want to defragment, and the program will do the rest. At the end, it will show you a nice report, of how many files are defragmented, and how much percent your computer's performance is increased.

The registery, is only left for the clean and speed up tutorial

This freeware program, is also from Auslogics, but this time, it's the registery optimizer (download it here.

Select next, all the time, until its busy, now wait, and when it's done. Your computer will be faster

Restart your computer

We are done with cleaning it up, if you never did this before to your computer, keep the software on your computer, and schedule Registery and Disk defrag monthly, and Ccleaner once a week. You will keep it fast this way.

But, how about viruses, and malicious ware that slows done our computers? In this part, i will give you some software, to prevent malicious ware, for free.

Viruses first

If you are not really known with the computer, you only know the virusses part, but there are more, spyware and malicious ware. But, we will install all the needed software, to prevent, and scan and delete the malicious software on your computer.

Grisoft AVG Free Edition

My favourite free anti virus, this will update everyday automatic, and prevents viruses, trojans and more. It gives you realtime protection, and scans your computer daily (you can fix this tho)

Download AVG Antivirus here

As soon as you get it install it, no computer reboot is neccesary, it has a nice tray icon, and most of the time, it will update after installation.

Open up AVG then click on Test Center, and then click on Scan Computer.
This might take a while, it will show all the viruses it founded, and after the scan, it will ask your commands. Most of the time, it's move to Virus Chest.
