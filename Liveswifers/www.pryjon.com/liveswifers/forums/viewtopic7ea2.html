Update: Deadline extended into 2008!
I was thinking of Halloween this year when I remembered the Halloween flash contest here last year. I said to myself, "Well, it's too late this year for a Halloween flash contest, but why not do a winter flash contest?"
I propose a Winter '07 Flashing contest. Here is the basic outline:
Rules:

  1. Flash file must be made in LiveSwif (any version is fine)
  2. Must be about winter (holidays, like Christmas and Hanukkah, are allowed)
  3. Art can be made outside of LiveSwif, and there will be no penalty or bonus if you do
  4. Sound/Music may be used, the only requirement is that it isn't obtained illegally. You can use your own sound/music, and there will be no penalty or bonus if you do
  5. Collabs are allowed, but credit everyone involved

Deadline:
The deadline will be January 12th, 2008. On the 13th, a poll will be available for about a week to vote. The winners will be announced on January 20th, 2008.
Notes:

  • Since it is the beginning of November, you have more than enough time for this project. This is set up this way so you have a lot of time to make your project.
  • At the moment, only flash movies and games will be accepted. Whether or not flash-made websites will be accepted is up for disscusion. Edit: Flash-Made Websites are now accepted
  • Feel free to ask question about exact times, considering people here are in all different timezones. I will try and make the stop time for accepting things midnight (GMT).
