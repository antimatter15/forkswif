How do you use the included preloader?
First, inport the liveswif movie by going to file>import>liveswif movie. The preloader should be under the path:

C:\Program Files\Liveswif\Liveswif 2.2\sample\intermediate\preloader.lmv

Then import it.

Once you finished that DELETE scene 1.
Then go to main (scene), then under the timeline, go to frame 2.
Now, change the text, delte it, or add your own.

Hope that helped!
