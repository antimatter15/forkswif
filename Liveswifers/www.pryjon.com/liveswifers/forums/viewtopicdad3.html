yes, because the driver converts the signals from the tablet into normal mouse positions. GIMP has some issues with the included mouse, but they can be fixed with the setting I spoke of earlier (I can direct you to a page on GIMPTalk where I talked about it. username:brwainer).


I almost forgot. WACOM has a policy that states it will include a version of Photoshop with all tablets. The Graphire BT tablets come with Elements 2, and the Intuos and Bamboo ones come with Elements 3. The Cintiq ones include Elements 4. They all come with Corel Painter Essentials 3 and Nik Color Efex™ Pro 2 IE from Nik Software.

To choose a tablet, I recommend you decide what your budget will be first, then what product line you want, then what size. I recommend the Bamboo or Cintiq ones. The best one is the Cintiq, because you actually draw on a 1600x1200 pixel screen.
