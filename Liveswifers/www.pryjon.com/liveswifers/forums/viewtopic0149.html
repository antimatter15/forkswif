Also. OpenSwif and LiveSwif are not the same thing!!!!! No 'bug' in LiveSwif will be fixed in OpenSwif, because OpenSwif will not have had that bug in the first place! OpenSwif will have its own bugs. everybody please stop saying that bugs in LiveSwif will be fixed in OpenSwif: that is not true at all and it makes people who come here think that LiveSwif is somehow still under development and its not.

Also. Hit state is the area that is clickable of the button. If you have a button with a hit state that is really tiny, but the button looks huge, users will have a hard time finding the right place to click, and likewise, if the hit state is too large or the wrong shape, it can really mess with the user. The best thing to do is to copy the down state and paste it into the hit state. Just be aware that the hit state is treated as invisible by flash.
