Microsoft has just put out some new web design software called Microsoft Web Expressions. It is supposed to be pretty good (but what do I know? I am only in the middle of the install right now!) You can download the beta here: http://www.microsoft.com/downloads/details.aspx?FamilyId=BCE55ABC-01B1-4402-B739-B8C3AABF00BF&displaylang=en
I'll give you review of the software after I try it out.

Notes:
This is a beta version, it is not yet a commercial product.
This is a trial version, it works until March 2007.
It is not a flash program, and probably has nothing to do with flash. It is a web design software, and focuses on HTML, ASP.net, XML, and CSS.
The download is 199MB, about 1000 minutes to download on dial-up! Do not attempt to download without a high speed connection!

OK, well have fun!
