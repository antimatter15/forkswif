There are 3 other editors, 9 other announcers, and 2 other cameramen (We all have to be an editor, announcer, and cameraman at least once a year). The announcers take turns doing the different parts of the show each day (It's not live) and we all do voiceovers, which is just a PowerPoint that we read. So, in any given show, You might see a 1/3 of the cast, hear 2/3 of them, and see/hear/read on t.v. the production of around 18-20 students. Our teacher decided to give his copies to the editors, so we can do our jobs better.
