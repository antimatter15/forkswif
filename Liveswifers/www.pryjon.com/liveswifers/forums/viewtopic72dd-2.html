If you feel like Flash is too much for you, there is not much I or anyone else could say to help you. Flash (& LiveSwif) is a great way to learn alot of computer science principles in a fun way: drawing, beziers, layers, game theory, visual design, programming, object orientation, encapsulation, client-side vs. server-side... all while making fun games and movies. You won't get all of this by moving to a "game maker" program, you will only get a few of them. The nice thing about Flash is that if you can't (or don't want to) learn programming, there is still a huge world of graphic design at you finger tips. Not much of an artist? There is still the programming, and there is also the fact that you can make movies with simple stick figure sprites and astound the world with you cleverness in the movie plot/storyline.

Maliq, I am not going to vote. You have to be the one to decide for yourself. I personally think that if you are looking to have fun learning and doing stuff on the computer, it is tough to beat Flash creation (using LiveSwif or whatever). By posting your creations as SWF files, they are available to the whole world. EXE files, first of all only work on MS Windows, and second of all, many people will not download them because they could contain viruses. I would be willing to try 200 flash files before downloading even one EXE game.

If you leave, Maliq, it will be your own loss.
