Well, you can use loadMovie() one of two ways:
Code:
loadMovie("filename.jpg", pictureFrame);
or
Code:
pictureFrame.loadMovie("filename.jpg");
Where "pictureFrame" is the "instance name" of a sprite on the current scene which you want to replace with the photo.
