We don't have anything. No proof that it's even has a GUI.

There are no known releases, public or otherwise. The project is at the state to be quite arguably considered vaporware.

If progress on OpenSwif does however occur, but at the best rate that the proejct has experienced, we may have a decent non-functioning GUI by 2009. Likely the ability to draw by 2010, animation by 2011 and flash export by 2013, possibly ActionScript by 2015.

Meanwhile, you can try using the OpenDialect, Salsalaga, Forkswif, or Ajax Animator projects, as they seem to be the only actively developed projects. None of them truly close to that of Flash (Though OpenDialect is soemwhat close).
