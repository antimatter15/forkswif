Is the preloader a separate scene? If so, you might want to combine it into one scene. The easiest way to do that is to start the making of the movie with the preloader. You're other options, if you already made the movie, is one of the following:
1. Move all the frames in your movie forward by one and put the preloader in the first frame.
2. Put the preloader in your movie as a separate scene, then remove the scene that doesn't have the preloader, and build the movie from the scene that's left.

The preloader code is not designed to work across multiple scenes.

If this is not your problem, please give us some more detail.
