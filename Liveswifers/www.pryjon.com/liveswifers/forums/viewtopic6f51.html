I feel the number of collab(oration)s are at a dangerous low of zip. I have therefore decided that a collab(oration) lounge should be setup for people to discuss possible collab(oration)s, join existing collab(oration)s, or talk about developing collab(oration)s. If at any time, your collab(oration) has matured to be in its own topic, feel free to do so. There is binding rule keeping you here. This is just a place to get one started.

The reason why I came up with this is because I was thinking: what if some people didn't use our program for this reason:
"Sure, it seems like a great program, but is it easy to organize a collab(oration)?"
I just feel there is better success if we had some collab(oration)s. After all, do you see a single collab(oration) done with Liveswif 2.2? What do you think?
---
When I get a handle on including other flashes in a flash file (will do a test soon enough), I will strongly consider accepting "applications" for WinFAuX. Credits will be awarded for these "applications".
