I believe we should have more contests, to have something to do with our awesome skills! Which is why I declare a summer contest, with summer just around the corner.

Rules

  • It MUST be created in LiveSwif. Any version will do fine. Think we won't be able to tell? We have ways of finding out...
  • It can be anything related to summer. You can even do something unrelated to summer, as long as it is something that can happen during the summer. An example of disqualification would be a meet Santa event at the mall.
  • You must also send a 48x48 picture with (not in) your project representing the entry. This is for a special flash file (created after the deadline) that will link to all the entries. This makes it easier to find them all.
  • The entry must be a .swf file. Ideally, the entry should link to a web page with the project embedded.
  • It must be a movie or a game. A liveswif-made website can be done, but the condition is that the liveswif-made website must be about summer.

Deadline
It must be sumbitted by the midnight (GMT) between July 4th and July 5th. A reference chart is below, showing the deadline time for the timezone you picked on the board (.5 times not shown):
