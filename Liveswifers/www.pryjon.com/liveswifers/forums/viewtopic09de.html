I just checked, and here are the different levels:


  • 'L'=0
  • 'LI'=15
  • 'LIV'=25
  • 'LIVE'=50
  • 'LIVES'=75
  • 'LIVESW'=100
  • 'LIVESWI'=150
  • 'LIVESWIF'=200
  • 'LIVESWIFE'=250
  • 'LIVESWIFER'=300
  • '++LIVESWIFER++'=400
  • '!!!LIVESWIFER!!!'=500
  • 'Insane'=1000
